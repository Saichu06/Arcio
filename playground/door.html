Concepts Applied
Ultrasonic Sensor
HC-SR04 emits 40kHz sound pulses and measures echo return time to calculate distance with centimeter precision.
Distance Measurement
Distance (cm) = (echo pulse duration × speed of sound) ÷ 2. The controller evaluates this against a programmable threshold.
Automation
A microcontroller continuously polls the sensor and triggers the door motor drive circuit when the threshold is crossed.
Smart Access
The door automatically closes after the person leaves the detection zone, completing a full open-close access cycle.
