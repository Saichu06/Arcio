Concepts Applied
Gas Sensors
MQ-series gas/smoke sensors continuously sample air for combustible or hazardous concentrations and report PPM levels.
Safety Monitoring
A controller continuously evaluates sensor thresholds against safe operating limits to catch hazards before they escalate.
Emergency Response
Detection triggers a coordinated response — automatic valve shutoff, audible/visual alarms, and forced ventilation.
Automation Logic
Microcontroller logic sequences each safety action — detect, alarm, ventilate — without requiring human intervention.
